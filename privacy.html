Privacy Policy for XY LOCK

Last updated: July 31, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how XY LOCK collects and uses limited information from users who interact with the bot in Discord servers.

2. What Data We Collect

XY LOCK collects only the minimum data required to operate properly:

  • Server IDs (to identify server-specific settings)
  • User IDs (to assign roles such as team manager or administrator)
  • Emoji IDs (to link custom emojis with specific commands)

XY LOCK does not collect messages, usernames, email addresses, IP addresses, or any personally sensitive information.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

The data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To store league and team configurations
  • To associate users with their roles or teams
  • To provide bot functionality like assigning roles or showing standings

4. Data Storage and Retention

Data is stored securely on our hosting provider and retained only as long as necessary. When a server removes XY LOCK, all related data is automatically removed within 30 days.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Data is only accessed by the bot itself to provide the intended functionality.

6. User Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

If you are a user from the European Union or a region covered by data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data

To make such a request, please contact us by email (see section 8).

7. Children's Privacy

XY LOCK is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.
